This line chart shows the trend of fish landings by British vessels in England and Wales from 1889 to 2007. From 1889 to 1911, the landings generally increased, followed by a sharp drop to a minimum of 140085 tons around 1917 then a recovery to 482936 tons in 1920. There was a fluctuating rise until 1937 of 677365 tons, then another sharp decline by 1941 of 115060 tons. Afterward, the landings recovered by 1949, then fluctuated downward until 2007 despite an occasional rise to 516406 tons in 1969.
