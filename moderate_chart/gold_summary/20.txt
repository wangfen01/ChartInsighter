This line chart shows the trend of fish landings by British vessels in the United Kingdom from 1889 to 2007. From 1889 to 1911, the landings generally increased year by year, followed by a sharp drop to a minimum of 194439 tons in 1917. Then it recovered to 642334 tons in 1920. There was a fluctuating rise until 1937, then another sharp decline to 187599 tons in 1941. Afterward, the landings recovered by 1950 to the levels seen in the 1930s, but then fluctuated downward until 1984 of 395028 tons, and then stabilizing somewhat at around 1 million.
