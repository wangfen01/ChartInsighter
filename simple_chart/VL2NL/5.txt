This visualization is a line chart that depicts the unemployment rate in Tokyo by gender over time. The data is plotted with the year on the horizontal axis and the unemployment rate on the vertical axis. Lines of different colors represent male and female genders, as indicated by the chart's legend. When a user hovers over the chart, a tooltip will appear, providing additional information about the year, unemployment rate, and gender for the point under the cursor. In the visualization, the peak unemployment rate for males in Tokyo was 3.9%, while females experienced their lowest unemployment rate at 2.2%. The year 2021 stands out as having the largest gender disparity in unemployment rates within the observed timeframe.
