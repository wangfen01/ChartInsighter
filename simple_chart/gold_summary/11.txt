The chart shows a line graph of coal consumption over time for continental Europe and India. 

Coal consumption in Europe was high for a long time from 1965 to 1989, fluctuating at over 9000 TWh, but it has declined significantly since then. It kept falling to 2950 TWh in 2023.

At the same time, India's coal consumption has been growing steadily, from 413 TWh in 1965 to 6105 TWh in 2023, with only one dip in 2020.

India has industrialized and is proliferating, in 2016, it surpassed Europe in terms of coal consumption and is still growing, while coal consumption in continental Europe is still declining.
