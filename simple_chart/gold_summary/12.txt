This chart shows a line graph of coal consumption in North America and India over time. 

Coal consumption in North America had been high for a long time and showed a growing trend from 3421 TWh in 1965 until 6837 TWh in 2007 century, followed by a steady decline to 2486 in 2023.

At the same time, India's coal consumption has been growing steadily, from 413 TWh in 1965 to 6105 TWh in 2023, with only one dip in 2020.

While North America continued to lead India since 1965, in 2005, India's coal consumption surpassed that of North America and continued to grow, while North America continued to decline.
