This chart shows how obesity rates have changed over time in the UK, Nigeria, and Japan. In terms of time, obesity rates have steadily risen in all three countries from 1975 to 2016.
The UK has a higher proportion of obese people and is rising faster from 40% in 1974 to 64% in 2016. Nigeria was the lowest of 9% at the very beginning. It overtook Japan in 2011, and by 2016, its obesity rate was 29%. Japan rose from 15% in 1975 to 27% in 2016. While the UK outpaced the other countries by more than 20%.
