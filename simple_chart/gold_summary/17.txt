This line graph shows how the stocks of AAPL (Apple) changed from 2000 to 2010.  AAPL's stock slightly fluctuated at the very beginning, then fell to 7.44 in December 2000. Then it stayed stable until 2004 and started to rise to a peak of 198.08 in December of 2007. Then it fell in 2008, however quickly recovered to before. It suddenly dropped to 85.35 in late 2008. And it rose to a new high of 223 in 2010.
