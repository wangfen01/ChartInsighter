This chart represents a line graph of air passenger traffic in China and Japan over time.   China's air passenger traffic grew from 710,000 in 1974 to 659 million in 2019, surpassing Japan in 2004.   Japan slowly increased from 16.32 million passengers in 1970 to 130.23 million in 2019 despite little dips between 2005 and 2011.   China has shown sustained growth even during the global crisis.   China at first lagged behind but took the lead since 2004.   2020 witnessed a decline of 36.7% in China and 60.7% in Japan.   In 2021, China grew by 5.5%, and Japan declined by 11.2%.
